MISSION

About Us

We are Commited to offer high quality education

Education is essential to the economic, social, environmental, and political well-being of an individual, the community and the nation. All individuals should be treated with dignity and respect, given opportunities to develop their special aptitudes and insights, and afforded equal opportunities to equip themselves for a fulfilling life and responsible citizenship.

We at RS Collegeare committed to offer high quality education at an affordable cost at the Pre-University, Undergraduate and Postgraduate levels, Our highly qualified faculty, rigorous academic programs and vibrant atmosphere provide the perfect place for you to continue your education.

The RS Group of Institutions has established many courses during the last 10 years. New courses are added every academic year as per the demand from the student community as well as industry. The courses, syllabus and our teaching methodology are updated from time to time as per as the guidelines of the university and other academic bodies.

The undergraduate and postgraduate dual program courses prepare you for the competitive world ahead. Our industry specific additional programs add a cutting edge to the syllabus.

Driven by commitment and passion, our goal is to create the citizens of tomorrow armed with every necessary skill set to take on every new challenge.

Apart from the focussed academic structure, RS Collegedevelops and hones your skills with debates, seminars and guest faculty lectures, Regular interaction with industry is an integral part of your study at RS College.

Sporting activities are not left behind either. You can choose from football, table tennis, throwball, basketball among many others. Driven by the vision of our Chairman and the relentless passion of our directors, RS Collegewelcomes you and holds hands with you in your quest of knowledge.
